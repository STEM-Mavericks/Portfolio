Our Project

Smart Dustbin

The Smart Dustbin enhances environmental cleanliness by automating waste management. This system is designed to make waste sorting easy, efficient, and reliable, helping users contribute to a cleaner world.

App Link

Check out our application: Visit our App WasteX

Key Features

  • Smart Waste Sorting: Automatically separates dry and wet waste with precision using moisture sensors.
  • Real-Time Monitoring: Keep track of bin levels and waste weight with real-time data, accessible via a web application.
  • Overload Prevention: The system monitors bin capacity with an ultrasonic sensor to avoid overflows.
  • Sustainability Support: Encourages efficient recycling by providing detailed waste analytics, promoting environmental sustainability.
  • Durability: Built for various environments—from humid to arid conditions—ensuring longevity and reliability.

System Components

  • Sensors:
    • Moisture Sensor: Differentiates dry from wet waste.
    • Load Sensor: Measures waste weight for tracking.
    • Ultrasonic Sensor: Tracks bin capacity, preventing overflow.
  • Actuators:
    • Servo Motor: Facilitates waste segregation by moving waste to the right compartment.

How It Works

  • Moisture Detection: Sensors detect the moisture content of the waste.
  • Sorting: Actuators segregate waste into the right compartment based on moisture levels.
  • Weight Measurement: The system tracks the weight of waste in real-time.
  • Capacity Monitoring: The ultrasonic sensor monitors bin levels and alerts users when the bin is full.

Environmental Impact

  • Reduces human error in waste sorting and encourages recycling.
  • Prevents bin overflow and keeps public spaces clean.
  • Contributes to sustainable waste management practices.
  • Achieving Sustainable Development Goals.
